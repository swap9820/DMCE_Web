Computer Engineering

The Computer Engineering department was established in 1992 with an intake of 60 for the under graduate course and further it was increased to 90 and 120 in the year 2000 and 2001 respectively. Masters in Computer Engineering was introduced in 2010 with the intake of 18 and in 2013 opened the research centre to run Ph. D. course with 15 intake under the University of Mumbai. In the view of quality education, department facilitates well-equipped laboratories and highly prominent and dedicated faculties having expertise in desperate field of technology. To make the students stand in the recent trends and technology, department conducts various Skill Development Programs for the students under the association CATT (Computer Association for Technological Trends) formed by the students. To bridge the industry requirement, CSI-DMCE student chapter which was inaugurated in 2012 conducts various workshops, seminars and technical competitions. Faculty members of the department are actively involved in research and have published more than 425 papers in renowned International Journals and Conferences in India and abroad. For their continuous growth and development in the field of technology, faculty actively participates in various STTPâ€™s, workshops and seminars. Department has a history of producing meritorious students every year, and are also awarded scholarships from prestigious trusts.The department has achieved excellent placement records from reputed companies like TCS, INFOSYS, L&T etc. Students are involved in pursuing higher education in India and abroad. Many faculty members are pursuing Ph.D. program and highly active in research activities. Large number of technical and research papers authored by the faculty members, research scholars and PG students are published in the peer reviewed and reputed journals of national and international levels indexed in prominent data bases such as Web of Science (SCI/SCIE/ESCI), SCOPUS and Google Scholar. Besides, they also actively participate in the conferences at national and international levels. Experts from various industries along with alumni have been invited for interactive discussions with students and have delivered the expert talks through different programs. In all, the department leads in all fronts for overall growth for both students and faculty.

The department was established in the year 1992. Research lab for the Ph.D. program in computer engineering is approved from 2013-14. Department has started M.E. program in 2010.

Laboratories are well equipped with computers having latest configuration, and provides application software such as MatLab, Rational Rose Java ,VB.net, ASP.net, Visual basic, C,C++ and database software such as Oracle and SQL Server. Department has independent library with more than 300 books.

Faculties have published more than 150 papers at international & national conferences.

CSI DMCE student chapter inaugurated in the year 2012-13. CATT (Computer Association for Technological Trend), a student association actively organizes various technical & non technical events such as technical test, group discussion, code debugging, mock test, technical seminar on advance technologies.

Final year result for 2013-14 is 93.71% with 60 students securing distinction. M.E result for academic year 2013-14 is 77.78%. Bagged 8th and 9th position in university merit list of the final year 2013-14
